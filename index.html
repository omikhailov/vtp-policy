Publisher is not responsible for your actions and actions of Microsoft Corporation or device manufacturers in the case when such actions give him access to personal data.

Trusted services

For the purpose of cover art searching Application may send song title, artist name, album title, album year and other metadata to following trusted services:

  • musicbrainz.org
  • coverartarchive.org
  • last.fm
  • discogs.com

In addition, these services may receive information about your IP address and preferred language. Application will share your information with third parties only in the way that is described in this privacy statement.

You can disable cover art search feature in Application settings.

Application may use certain APIs of last.fm and Last.fm Ltd may have access to your data and information about Application usage if you will enable integration with Last FM. Such APIs are subject of your agreements with Last.fm Ltd and Publisher is not responsible for them. You can disable integration with Last FM using settings page on https://www.last.fm/

Such APIs are subject of your agreements with Deezer SA and Publisher is not responsible for them. You can disable integration with Deezer using settings page on https://www.deezer.com/
